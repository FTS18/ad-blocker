Active Privacy Shields

The blocker automatically runs these filters locally on your browser:

  • Ad Blockers: Blocks standard display advertisements.
  • YouTube Shield: Skips video commercial ads instantly and closes overlays.
  • Tracker Blocker: Blocks telemetry, analytics, and user profiling.
  • Malware Shield: Prevents connections to scam and malicious sites.
  • Cookie Guard: Intercepts and blocks writes to tracking cookies.
  • WebRTC Shield: Mitigates local IP address disclosures.
  • Link Cleaner: Automatically strips outbound smartlink query tags.
