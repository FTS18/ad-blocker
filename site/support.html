Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I open the Settings Dashboard?
A: Right-click the extension icon in your browser toolbar and select "Options", or click the "Dashboard" button at the bottom of the extension popup window.
Q: How do I whitelist a trusted website?
A: Open the extension popup while visiting the website you want to trust, and turn off the "this website" toggle. Alternatively, you can add domains manually on the Options Dashboard.
Q: Can I restore an element that I blocked by accident?
A: Yes! Open the Options Dashboard and navigate to the "Custom Cosmetic Filters" section at the bottom. You can view all blocked CSS selectors, delete the one you blocked by accident, and click "Save CSS Filters".
Q: Does this extension block YouTube ads?
A: Yes. The blocker features an optimized YouTube ad skipping engine that speeds up and skips video ads instantly, closes overlays, and automatically dismisses ad blocker warning dialogs.
