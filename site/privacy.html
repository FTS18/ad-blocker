Trust & Privacy

Privacy Policy of AdVoid

Your privacy is not for sale. Learn about our local-first, zero-data-collection philosophy for web and YouTube browsing.

1. Local-First Philosophy

This extension is built to run entirely inside your browser. All ad-blocking calculations, whitelist evaluations, custom rulesets, and blocked counter statistics happen locally on your computer. We do not operate remote tracking servers, and your data never leaves your device.

2. Zero Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data. Specifically, we do not log:

  • Websites you visit or URLs you load.
  • Your IP address, location, or ISP information.
  • Blocked request statistics (which remain saved strictly in your local browser storage).
  • Browser metadata, hardware profiles, or device settings.

3. Third-Party Requests

When the extension blocks an ad or tracker network, it cancels the network connection entirely. This prevents third-party servers (like ad exchanges or tracking pixels) from reading or writing cookies, logging your IP address, or tracking you across different websites.

4. Storage Usage

The extension utilizes Chrome's local storage API (chrome.storage.local) to store your preferences, whitelisted domains, and custom elements hidden via the picker tool. This storage is managed by your browser and is deleted automatically if you uninstall the extension.

5. Changes to This Policy

Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not have a mailing list or accounts to notify you of updates. Any changes to our privacy practices will be documented directly on this page. We encourage you to review it periodically.
